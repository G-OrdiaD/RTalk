Thank you for installing {{ .Chart.Name }}.

Your application has been deployed.

To access your frontend:
{{- if .Values.ingress.enabled }}
  1. Find the Ingress IP or hostname:
     kubectl get ingress -n {{ .Release.Namespace }}
  2. Visit the URL in your browser.
{{- else }}
  1. Get the frontend service port:
     kubectl get service {{ include "chat-app-chart.fullname" . }}-frontend -n {{ .Release.Namespace }}
  2. Visit the following URL in your browser (it may take a few moments for the port to be assigned):
     http://localhost:{{ .Values.frontend.service.port }}
{{- end }}

To check the backend status:
  - Run: kubectl get pods -l app.kubernetes.io/name={{ include "chat-app-chart.name" . }}-backend
  - View the logs: kubectl logs -f <backend-pod-name>
